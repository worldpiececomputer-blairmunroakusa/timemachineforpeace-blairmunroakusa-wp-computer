Everything here is highly experimental. SAFETY: These ideas can be very stimulating. Please do not let exploring these ideas come at the cost of complete destabilization in your world. If you need to take a break, take a break. If you ever feel like you are losing stability, at risk for complete destabilization, please consider seeking out professional help immediately.

11/04/2021 - The contents of this website were created under distress, for at the time I was undergoing an acute psychotic episode, product of a condition called 'schizoaffective disorder, bipolar type'. I have since stabilized, but I have chosen to leave this website up as a personal reminder to self. I still believe in the general idea I was attempting to convey, and all ideas associated with this communication attempt (eg, the kickstarter), but overall, my ability to do so in an accessible and relatable manner was impaired. I can do better. I am currently in the process of creating a more accessible version of this website and the ideas contained within. For now however, it stands as-is. --Blair

The primary problem with my delivery was that I included a bunch of personal stuff, content better suited for publication on my personal subdomain blairmunroakusa.wp.compmuter. timemachineforpeace.wp.computer should only contain information outlining the overall social invention project. Only the highest level aspects of the unifying strategy I am attempting to convey. --Blair

NOTE REGARDING THE UNIVERSAL PEACE PLAN:

timemachineforpeace.wp.computer will be a primary subdomain within The Universal Peace Plan, which will for now be a collection of subdomains within the wp.computer (world piece computer) and up.computer (universal peace computer) domain space. For practical purposes, The Universal Peace Plan will be accessible via theuniversalpeaceplan.wp.computer and theuniversalpeaceplan.up.computer, which will serve as a subspace to index the subdomains relating to the Time Machine For Peace, and the plan for turning it into a sustainable social invention project.

Some examples: blairmunroakusa.wp.computer and blairmunroakusa.up.computer is a subspace hosting the image of my personal world piece computer. growth.wp.computer and growth.up.computer will be a subspace for outlining the growth strategy and plan within The Universal Peace Plan. The apex subdomains apx.wp.computer and apx.up.computer will form a subspace devoted to general attributes underlying world piece computers and The Universal Peace Computer.

blairmunroakusa.wp.computer is a cross-transparency.
